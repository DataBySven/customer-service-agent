### 1\. Introduction

It ensures consistent investigation and resolution of customer claims regarding undelivered orders.

### 3\. Open Customer Profile

Review the customer’s message carefully — these cases typically begin when tracking shows “Delivered”, but the customer reports they did not receive the parcel.

![Open Customer Profile](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2F2dYXVYwS8wROAv1uzW7HGrg3ckF2%2FrEU5SgVFpW4NXw23WBTZP4%2FksF4cuagERkU4BV6dDbWEQ_doc.png?alt=media&token=c75e9169-1821-4e22-b23d-c9ed0af4b1bd)

### 4\. Open Customer Profile

Customer contacted us on 18th November.

![Open Customer Profile](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2F2dYXVYwS8wROAv1uzW7HGrg3ckF2%2FrEU5SgVFpW4NXw23WBTZP4%2Fa3U3pemh6SpB8Zo4X36QLB_doc.png?alt=media&token=04c6277e-018a-4811-95d0-bda67af52c78)

### 5\. Select Delivery Process Email

Verify the claim by checking tracking updates in Service Point or 17Track. Upon review on Service point, this was delivered on 8th of November.

![Select Delivery Process Email](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2F2dYXVYwS8wROAv1uzW7HGrg3ckF2%2FrEU5SgVFpW4NXw23WBTZP4%2FgLV1LgrULPjZcAeunh755F_doc.png?alt=media&token=13c27c0c-61eb-4498-864c-f6567187ddba)

### 6\. Open Dispute History

Since this is a possible lost in transit, we will file a dispute via service point to a confirm the initial finding that indeed this is a lost in transit to ask for a proof of delivery. We are expecting to receive this for 24 hours. If supplier, cannot provide for Proof of delivery, we will request for a replacement then refund if customer would insist. Please make sure to track it on Dispute Tracker on Clickup

![Open Dispute History](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2F2dYXVYwS8wROAv1uzW7HGrg3ckF2%2FrEU5SgVFpW4NXw23WBTZP4%2FvScN86NGhpnXFc95BJvPmq_doc.png?alt=media&token=eee14a19-4c0e-414b-b215-db4a5e532ebd)

Switch back to Gorgias. Step 3. Send the message to the customer using Macro and polish message on ChatGPT now that we have an initial findings that this is a possible lost in transit.
