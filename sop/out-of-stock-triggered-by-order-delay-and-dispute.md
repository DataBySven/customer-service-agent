### 13\. Select Shipment Information

Check on the latest update and take of the date. The last update was on 10th November versus the email sent by customer dated on 3rd December. This is almost a month and out of SLA. File a dispute to find out what is going on. Do not ask for a refund since customer has not ask for one yet.

![Select Shipment Information](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2F2dYXVYwS8wROAv1uzW7HGrg3ckF2%2F6bisZ6k6Aj2ZKME1sSanXf%2Fh1V9W4JTw6DCLuMGVmLYVT_doc.png?alt=media&token=b6f070ef-1451-449b-9391-bfabfebabf3e)

### 14\. Click Link Here

Step 4: Review the current status: usually out-of-timeline results lead to submitting a dispute in Service Point; Once the supplier responds and confirms the item is out of stock, proceed to Offer to the customer a different variation if available so can resend and if not, proceed with a refund. Track the Dispute and Refund in Dispute Tracker and Refund Tracker on Click-up

### 19\. Click Snooze Keyboard

Step 7. Snooze the ticket for 5 days to allow time for the refund to appear in the customer’s bank account.

![Click Snooze Keyboard](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2F2dYXVYwS8wROAv1uzW7HGrg3ckF2%2F6bisZ6k6Aj2ZKME1sSanXf%2FnJ5zezSbj9dfDxfjFtGmD3_doc.png?alt=media&token=98c4aed2-efe4-49be-aa5e-227cdadef58c)

After 5 days, close the ticket using the appropriate closing macro series once the customer confirms that the refund has been received or has no complaints. If due for resend, continue monitoring the order for Proactive Order Delay Follow-up.
