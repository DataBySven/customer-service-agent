### 1\. Introduction

Step 1. Understand the issue by reading the customer’s email and select Customer Clearance as the contact reason.

![Introduction](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2F2dYXVYwS8wROAv1uzW7HGrg3ckF2%2F8b4Sfa3MGPCv8uJp48T4yE%2Frug84KqdpVscZWqgQKLG9E_doc.png?alt=media&token=81c73ab5-dc04-4b8c-83e3-4c35b8961dc9)

### 2\. Open Customer Details

Read and understand the email. Upon review, the customer mentioned about a communication to her that she was advise that she will be paying a custom or import charge. This happens seldom only like around 1% of our tickets or once every 3 months. Customs or the delivery company do not send this communication to us.
