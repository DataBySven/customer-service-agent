This procedure outlines the steps to proactively manage order delays to ensure timely customer communication and compliance. It applies to customer service representatives handling delayed orders using the Gorgias platform and related tools.

### Go to [northenoak.gorgias.com](https://northenoak.gorgias.com)

### 1\. Open Inbox

Step 1: Check your inbox from snoozed tickets, understand the issue, review previous internal notes, and confirm it is a follow-up order or a second order update; if you see a previous first-order status update, then continue — do not change the contact reason in the dropdown. You may go to your Inbox to check on tickets assigned to you previously ready for follow up after 3 days.

![Open Inbox](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2F2dYXVYwS8wROAv1uzW7HGrg3ckF2%2FaKCqVyceKXzmgdXqHsJQYu%2FjqsdfkzPLBiJrvYC8MUX2P_doc.png?alt=media&token=39c2832e-7272-4d19-94ca-84a72bc10bf2)

### 2\. Select All Messages

You can also find these tickets under ALL. ALL Inbox has the tickets snoozed and new messages that are also assigned to you and your teammates.

![Select All Messages](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2F2dYXVYwS8wROAv1uzW7HGrg3ckF2%2FaKCqVyceKXzmgdXqHsJQYu%2FqS55dSwk6AJ19qhgpsEHkv_doc.png?alt=media&token=95a1d5e4-846c-4cf3-8c18-0b6ac0ed28a0)

![Activate Snooze Feature](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2F2dYXVYwS8wROAv1uzW7HGrg3ckF2%2FaKCqVyceKXzmgdXqHsJQYu%2Fr6qZ9dGKEvnhA4acK7vHMA_doc.png?alt=media&token=3155309b-25f5-4ad2-a349-3eb3896b2623)
